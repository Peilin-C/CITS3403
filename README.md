# Study Buddy

A web application that helps university students find study partners, form project teams, and organise group study sessions.

## Project Idea

Study Buddy makes it easy for students to connect based on shared units, availability, and study preferences.  Whether you're looking for someone to revise with before exams or searching for teammates for a group project, Study Buddy Finder has you covered.

Users can create a profile with their units, availability, and study preferences, then browse and connect with other students.  The app also supports finding project teammates by allowing users to indicate if they are open to group work.

## Key Features

User Authentication — Sign up and log in securely
Student Profiles — Add your units, availability, and study preferences
Browse & Search — Find study buddies filtered by unit, time, or interest
Team Formation — Indicate openness to group work and find project teammates
Study Sessions — Create, browse, and join study sessions with other students
